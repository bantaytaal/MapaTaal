    MapaTaal aims to consolidate public geospatial data related to the recent Taal Volcano eruption.

    The platform is based on Leaflet, typeahead.js and BootLeaf.

    Current maps are from Phivolcs' GIS database found here

    For suggestions, feature requests and bug reporting, please email bantaytaal2020@gmail.com

    Phivolcs is the authoritative source of information on the Taal Volcano eruption.

    The data provided on MapaTaal is for general information purposes only. It is not connected to Phivolcs or any government agency.
